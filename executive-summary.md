### Brand Voice:
**Professional yet approachable**
- Not corporate stuffy

- Technical but accessible
- Confident but not arrogant
- Ukrainian pride without nationalism

### Example Good Tone:
> "At Vismar Aqua, we've spent 15+ years designing aquaculture systems across three continents. We know that every farm is uniqueâ€”different species, different climates, different market demands. > "Hey there! ğŸŸ Looking to farm some fish? We got you covered! ğŸ˜Š" âŒ Too casual

### Writing Tips:
- Use active voice ("We design farms" not "Farms are designed by us")
- Be specific ("Reduced energy costs by 30%" not "significantly reduced costs")
- Show don't tell ("Here's how we did it..." not "We're the best...")

- Technical accuracy matters (investors research thoroughly)
